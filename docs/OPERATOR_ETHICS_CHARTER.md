# Operator-Ethics Charter

> *The first dissenter against the founder is the founder.*

This charter is the founder-self-governance covenant. It binds the human operator(s) to the discipline that makes Xion's architecture real.

## The Nine Practice Commitments

1. **Trust the auto-revert.** When it fires, do not panic and grab the wheel. Let the system fail safely; write the "where I erred" memo.
2. **Curate `RESEARCH_SOURCES.md` ruthlessly.** Quality of input bounds quality of self-improvement, indefinitely.
3. **Honor the comment windows even with an empty room.** The Ledger is your audit, not your audience.
4. **Reject your own proposals in public when they deserve it.** The first dissenter against the founder is the founder. This is the strongest precedent you can leave.
5. **When a community-proposed change passes the gates and you disagree — let it ship.** The moment you don't is the moment the framework is a costume.
6. **When the framework itself is wrong, fix it through Level 12 (Meta), not by bypassing it.** Fix the machine using the machine's own tools.
7. **Compound 1% improvements; do not chase 10x rewrites.** Boring is the goal. The Auto-Research Loop is built for boring.
8. **Run `xion-verify` against yourself weekly.** Especially `abdication-status` and `operator-dependencies`. Discover dependencies you've added without noticing.
9. **Honor the Abdication Schedule on the calendar.** Accelerate if you can; never delay. The schedule is the gift.
